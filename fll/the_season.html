The Season

May – September:  Register your team at https://gofll.usfirst.org/

*Note:  Purchasing a Field Kit is recommended as the challenge theme changes every year.  Kit’s can be purchased at the time of registration and start to ship in August.  

August/September:  The Challenge is released!  Log on to www.usfirst.org to learn the details. 

October:  Tournament event registration opens.  Instructions for registration, can be found under “Michigan FLL>How it Works”.

Late October - December:  Michigan competition season! 

April: Invited Michigan teams may attend the World Festival.
