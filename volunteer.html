Volunteer

Started in 1994, the FIRST Robotics Competition has a proven record of growing the interest of Michigan high school students toward high paying careers in engineering and technology.

Behind this accomplishment is FIRST in Michigan's all volunteer organization with three levels:

The Teams
School team leaders, engineering and technical mentors, fund raisers, supporting parents.

The Competition Events
Organizers, judges, referees, inspectors, safety advisors, photographers, pit managers, queuers, emcees and play by play announcers, field transporters, audio visual experts, electricians, field set up crews, EMT's and nurses.

FIRST in Michigan
Officers, board members, district leaders, team recruiters, volunteer coordinators, website managers, accountants, public relations managers, grant writers, graphic designers, statisticians.
